Presentation

✧ In deep sea, there is a group of octopus, they form a special "society".

✧ Every octopus is unique, they have different "backgrounds", different "outfits", different "social status" and even different "characteristics".

✧ Just like you and me.

✧ Do you know you are irreplaceable? Do you feel comfortable in this community? What role do you play? What colour represents you? Which octopus gives you resonance?

✧ In this moment, we dive into deep sea, and to find another "US".
